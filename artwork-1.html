Eternity

about the project

“Eternity” is an audio-visual game trailer project with a primary focus on sound design, crafted to immerse viewers in its suspense-driven narrative. The game follows Jay, a local detective, as he investigates the mysterious disappearance of a close friend. The case is commissioned by another childhood companion, whose own perspective on the events may conceal deeper motives. As the investigation unfolds, Jay is drawn into a labyrinth of hidden truths, gradually uncovering a chilling and deceptive past that has shadowed him all his life. Through atmospheric soundscapes, layered audio cues, and precise timing, the trailer heightens tension and amplifies the emotional weight of each scene, allowing the audience to feel the unease, urgency, and revelations alongside the protagonist.
