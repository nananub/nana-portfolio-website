Flavors of Home

about the project

“Flavors of Home” is a 2D animatic project dedicated to raising awareness of the cultural and emotional significance of family meals. The narrative follows a humanized otter girl as she prepares supper for herself, her mind drifting back to cherished memories of cooking alongside her mother during childhood. These recollections evoke a spectrum of emotions such as nostalgia, warmth, and quiet joy, rekindling her appreciation for the moments they shared. Through its gentle storytelling and visual portrayal of Vietnamese traditions, the film celebrates the enduring value of family meals as both a cultural legacy and a means of strengthening familial bonds. By weaving together personal memory and cultural heritage, the work invites audiences to reflect on the simple yet profound connections built around the dining table.
