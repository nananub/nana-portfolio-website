Escape - Self portrait movie

about the project

"Escape" is a short self-portrait film that explores themes of illusion, fragmentation, and self-reflection through the use of three carefully composed camera shots. Employing blurriness, overlays, and experimental effects, the work creates a visual language of disorientation that symbolizes the sensation of an “escaping” inner self. Beneath its abstract imagery lies a metaphorical narrative on self-awareness, with each scene serving as a reflection of internal struggles and the search for identity. Through its interplay of visual distortion and symbolic composition, the film presents a meditation on self-perception, inviting viewers to engage with the complexities of self-reflection and the elusive nature of personal identity.
