Tam's Untold Story

about the project

“Tam's Untold Story” is a project that involves the design of a digital infographic for Dove Body Scrubs, presented through a narrative format. The concept draws inspiration from the Vietnamese fairytale Tam Cam, reimagined within the cultural and historical context of nineteenth- and early twentieth-century Vietnam during the Nguyen Dynasty. The scenario serves as a continuation of the original tale, depicting Tam as Queen alongside the King. Over time, however, the King introduces additional concubines into the palace, leaving Tam isolated and distressed. At this point, the Fairy Godfather intervenes, presenting Tam with a Dove bird as a symbol of comfort and salvation. The narrative integrates elements of Vietnamese heritage, including traditional architecture, attire, and cultural motifs, while the color palette and graphic style have been adapted to reflect the historical setting and thematic direction of the work.
