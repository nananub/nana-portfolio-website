Fetters of Reflective Petals

about the project

“Fetters of Reflective Petals” is a digital art project that merges 2D and 3D visual techniques to explore the human experience of self-awareness and introspection. It examines the emotional disconnect that arises when individuals fail to recognize or acknowledge their own feelings, resulting in uncertainty about personal goals, a weakened link between emotions and actions, and a reluctance to express ideas for fear of criticism or rejection. Such detachment often hinders meaningful connections with others and prevents engagement with one’s inner self. Centered on the concept of “Reflection” as a form of self-communication, the work invites viewers to consider self-awareness as a pathway toward deeper understanding of one’s identity and perspective on the world. The theme extends to related ideas of self-criticism, self-realization, and self-rumination, aiming to inspire college students to explore their potential, build trust in their own abilities, and nurture their inner child as part of a process of personal healing.
